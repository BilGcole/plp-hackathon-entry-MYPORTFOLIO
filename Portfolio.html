About Me

With a broad and dynamic career across multiple industries, I bring a wealth of experience and a unique skill set to every endeavor. Over the past seven years, I've developed a deep understanding of customer service and client engagement as a tour guide. This role has allowed me to refine the art of storytelling and connect with people from diverse backgrounds, creating memorable experiences and fostering lasting relationships.

In parallel, my journey as a Full Stack Developer has equipped me with the technical expertise to build and optimize a variety of web and mobile applications. From front-end design to back-end functionality, my passion for coding and problem-solving has kept me at the forefront of digital innovation. This technical acumen is at the heart of my role as the Founder and CEO of Zegär Technologies Ltd, where I lead the charge in developing cutting-edge solutions that blend security, sustainability, and user-centric design.

My experience extends beyond technology and tourism into the realms of supply chain management and the fast-moving consumer goods (FMCG) industry. This role in the FMCG sector was particularly impactful, as it provided me with a deep understanding of the complexities of global supply chains, demand forecasting, and cost management. My ability to streamline processes, reduce costs, and ensure the timely delivery of goods was instrumental in driving efficiency and profitability within the organizations I served.

These combined experiences in supply chain management, FMCG, technology, and tourism have not only broadened my perspective but have also equipped me with a comprehensive skill set that I bring to Zegär Technologies. Whether I am leading a team to innovate in the tech space, ensuring seamless operations in supply chain logistics, or enhancing customer experiences in tourism, I approach each challenge with a strategic mindset and a commitment to excellence. My diverse background allows me to navigate the complexities of various industries, making informed decisions that drive success and growth in all facets of the business.
